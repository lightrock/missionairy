Z0 Quark GeneZip Compression

This workbench stops counting random substring encounters and asks the compression question directly: can the actual quark/subsystem grammar cover CODATA constant bitstrings, what literal bits are left, and does that grammar beat fake quarks that are at least as long?

Compression Settings

GeneZip uses exact, non-overlapping copy-paste semantics. The score prefers fewer leftover literal bits, then fewer tokens, then longer quark words.
